Pollution

Pollution causing harm to us and wildlife

Section 1

In some countries like Bangladesh, Chad, Pakistan and many more are polluted. These countries are polluted because of car, motorbikes, bus that release exhaust fumes. Exhaust Fumes cause global warming and when inhale it cause respiratory issue. Exhaust Fume are poisonous carbon monoxide. https://waqi.info/#/c/31.093/56.069/3.4z

Section 2

When there is horrible air quality it can cause harm to the animal. The affects are disruption of endocrine function, organ injury, increased vulnerability to stresses and diseases, lower reproductive success, and possible death. Also when plastic reaches the ocean the fish, seabirds, sea turtles, and marine mammals can become entangled in or ingest plastic debris, causing suffocation and starvation. https://www.asyousow.org/our-work/circular-economy/plastic-pollution

Section 3

Pollution can cause Habitat shifting, where some animals are forced to flee where they live in order to survive, An increase in wildfires, due to polluted areas often becoming very dry, Increased air pollution, which burning waste contributes to and the water is polluted.
